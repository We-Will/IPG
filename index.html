We currently work with the following orphanages to provide the children with Food, Education, Healthcare, Clothing and upkeep of buildings.

  • 3 Orphanages in Srilanka 
    •  -Shakthi Girls Home
 -Hari Boys Home
 -Tikiri Sevena(Government Orphanage for Newborns)
  • 1 in Cambodia

  • 2 in Laos
